Shibuya Scramble Square



type :

Subway station, mixed-use, observation deck

location :

2-23 Shibuya Shibuya, Tokyo, Japan

completed :

2019 (East Tower); 2027

SHIBUYA SCRAMBLE SQUARE, a 230 meter tower overlooking the famous Shibuya Scramble CRossing. it is designed to embody the future of Shibuya, a dynamic, international, and ever-changing city. The high-rise building consists of Floors B2 to 16 have shops and restaurants that offer the latest trends in terms of fashion, lifestyle and dining in Tokyo. Floors 17 to 45 are occupied by offices of various companies.A major highlight is SHIBUYA SKY offering a fabulous 360-degree view of Tokyo and interactive exhibitions for visitors to feel the pulse of the city with all five senses.






SHIBUYA SKY:



Feel the Pulse of the City with All Five Senses




Of particular interest to visitors is SHIBUYA SKY, an observatory offering a 360-degree view over Tokyo along with interactive exhibitions that stimulate one's curiosity and imagination.
SHIBUYA SKY consists of three zones: SKY GATE - the entrance located on the 14F, SKY STAGE - the outdoor observation area on the rooftop (46F), and SKY GALLERY, and indoor observation and exhibition area (46F).

SKY STAGE:

View the Shibuya Scramble Crossing from the Highest Point!

The SKY STAGE (46F) offers an unobstructed 360-degree view of Tokyo From here, visitors can see other major Tokyo landmarks such as

the New National Stadium, Shinjuku, Tokyo Tower, Roppongi Hills, TOKYO SKYTREE, Tokyo Bay, and more! There's even a special spot to view and photograph Mt. Fuji from.

One of the corners overlooks the Shibuya Scramble Crossing.
The entire viewing experience is thrilling and lets you feel the dynamism and energy of Shibuya.
