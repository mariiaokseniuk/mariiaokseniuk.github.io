Explore hidden gems of Tokyo.




Sky Stage :

The view of Tokyo by night, especially the vibrant Shibuya, is an attraction in itself. The SKY STAGE takes it to another level, making the visitors realize they are a part of this stunning dynamic scenery.

Learn more >>>



Inokashira Park :

Inokashira Park is loved among locals for providing a luscious green space, with plenty of recreational facilities, a shrine, and a bucolic pond. Inokashira Pond is located in the center of the park, where you can hire rowing boats.

Learn more >>>



Anata no Warehouse :

Step off the streets of Kawasaki and into a dystopian amusement arcade. It’s part sci-fi, part dedicated re-make of the original Walled City of Kowloon.

Learn more >>>



Tsukiji Hongwanji:

Tsukiji Hongwanji Temple is another jewel in Tokyo's cityscape. This Buddhist temple is unlike any other, especially in terms of its architecture.
